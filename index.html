HCI Experiment with Professionals

Welcome to the HCI Experiment with Professionals.

This study, as part of a larger research project, aims to understand how experts (Data Scientists, Data Engineers, ML/AI Engineers, etc.) interact with AI. In this experiment you will first answer a presurvey, then engage in 5 tasks in which AI will assist you. After completing each task, you will answer 4 questions related to the task, and finally, you will complete a post-experiment survey.

This experiment is completely voluntary and anonymous. Your answers will be saved on the server after you complete experiment.
